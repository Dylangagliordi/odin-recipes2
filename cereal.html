The Perfect Bowl of Cereal

Description

A bowl of cereal is a staple meal that anyone can enjoy at any time, heres how you make the perfect bowl.

Ingredients

  • spoon
  • Bowl
  • Milk
  • Cereal of your choosing

Steps:

  1. Pour desired serving size of cereal into bowl
  2. Pour in your milk until it rises just enough to start floating the cereal
  3. Place spoon in bowl
  4. Enjoy!
